HOW TO USE

1) Clean and Dry the surface

2) Water into the bucket then add 20kg of xtreme Bond

3) Mix well & wait for 5 minutes

4) Spread the mixture on the surface & notch trowel

5) Spread the mixture at the backside of the tile and place on the surface

5) Evenly press the tile to maximize the bonding strength and then clean the surface
